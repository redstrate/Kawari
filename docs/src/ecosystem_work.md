# Ecosystem Contributions

Our project tries to be a good steward in the FFXIV open-source community,
mainly by upstreaming changes to other projects. A non-exhaustive list of
other projects we contributed to are:

* [FFXIVClientStructs](https://github.com/aers/FFXIVClientStructs)
* [EXDSchema](https://github.com/xivdev/EXDSchema)
* [Project Chronofoil](https://github.com/ProjectChronofoil)
* [TemporalStasis](https://github.com/NotNite/TemporalStasis)

We recommend our contributors to upstream changes when they can, because
Kawari is highly dependent on these projects. If you're drawing a blank
or just bored, you could:

* Upstream findings from packet structures to FFXIVClientStructs
* Fill out useful fields in EXDSchema

Are you really proud of something you accomplished with Kawari? [Let us
know](https://matrix.to/#/#kawari-welcome:pyra.sh)!
